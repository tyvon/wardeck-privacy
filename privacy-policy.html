War Deck Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 05/07/2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the War Deck application ("we", "our app") processes user information.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. War Deck is designed with the principle of minimal data collection.

Information Collection and Use

War Deck does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal user data.

Our application:

  • Does not require registration or authorization
  • Does not request access to personal information
  • Does not use advertising
  • Does not contain in-app purchases
  • Does not use analytics services
  • Does not require internet connection to function

Data Automatically Collected by Google Play

When downloading the app from Google Play, the app store may collect certain technical information in accordance with its privacy policy. We do not have access to this data.

Data Security

Since War Deck does not collect personal data, there are no risks associated with data breaches or unauthorized use.

Children's Use

War Deck is suitable for users aged 9 and above. The application does not collect any data from users of any age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
